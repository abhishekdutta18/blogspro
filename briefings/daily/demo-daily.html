Regulatory Pulse: SEBI's T+0 Expansion

SEBI today released a critical update regarding the optional T+0 settlement cycle. According to the document (Circular ID: SEBI/HO/MRD/192), the board intends to expand the list of eligible scrips from the current 25 to 100 by next month. This move is designed to enhance market liquidity and reduce counterparty risk for retail participants.
