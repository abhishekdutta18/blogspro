While they have been credited with improving market efficiency, their activities have also been linked to market volatility and the exacerbation of existing trends. As the global financial system continues to grapple with the Mythos threat, it is clear that high-frequency traders will play a critical role in shaping the outcome.

The central bank's move is likely to be seen as a signal that it is prioritizing stability over inflation control, at least for now. Meanwhile, India is tightening online content rules, which could have significant implications for the creator economy. The move is seen as a response to growing concerns about the spread of misinformation and the need for greater regulation of online content.
