Mythos Threat Looms: Global Financial System on the Brink

The remarkable renaissance of high-frequency traders is also a cause for concern. These traders have been making waves in the market with their lightning-fast trades, but their activities are also contributing to the volatility that is plaguing the global financial system.


The Hormuz disruption is another major concern, as traders warn of a potential global food shock. This could have devastating consequences for the global economy, particularly for countries that rely heavily on imports.

India is also tightening online content rules, which could have a significant impact on the creator economy. This move is seen as a step towards regulating the growing online content industry and ensuring that it is more accountable.


Finally, South Korea's Lee and India's Modi have agreed to deepen AI and chip ties. This move is seen as a major step towards strengthening the economic ties between the two countries and promoting innovation in the tech sector.
